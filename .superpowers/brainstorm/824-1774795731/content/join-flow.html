Cookie Reclaim Flow
If someone clears their cookies or switches devices:

1. Visit the group link
2. App sees no cookie → shows "Already a member?"
3. Tap their name from the member list
4. Confirm with a simple challenge (e.g. "What day did Benny stop?")
5. Cookie re-issued
